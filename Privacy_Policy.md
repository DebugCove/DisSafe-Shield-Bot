**Privacy Policy for DisSafe Shield Bot**

**Last updated:** 17 October 2024

**1. Introduction**

DisSafe Shield ("bot") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when using our bot on Discord.

By using the bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

**2. Information We Collect**

2.1. **User Data**: The bot may collect data related to Discord users, such as user IDs, roles, and usernames, which are necessary for its functionality (e.g., for moderating or punishing users as per server rules).

2.2. **Server Data**: We collect and store information about the server where the bot is used, including channel IDs, message content (for moderation purposes), and server settings that you configure.

2.3. **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage data such as interaction logs, error reports, and general activity statistics, which help us improve the bot’s performance and functionality.

**3. How We Use the Information**

3.1. **To Provide and Maintain the Service**: The collected data is used to ensure that the bot operates properly, offering moderation and other functionalities as described.

3.2. **To Improve the Bot**: Usage data and feedback help us identify bugs, enhance features, and develop new functionalities.

3.3. **Compliance with Legal Requirements**: In cases where we are required to do so by law, we may use your data to comply with regulatory requirements or to protect the rights and safety of others.

**4. Data Sharing**

4.1. **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or when necessary to maintain or protect the bot’s services (e.g., in case of abuse or security issues).

4.2. **Discord Integration**: As the bot operates within Discord, your data will naturally be processed by Discord’s servers as well. Please refer to Discord’s Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data.

**5. Data Retention**

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Data may be deleted when no longer needed, or at your request, unless there is a legal obligation to retain it.

**6. Your Rights**

6.1. **Access and Control**: You have the right to access the personal data we collect about you. If you believe any data is incorrect, you can request corrections.

6.2. **Data Deletion**: You may request the deletion of your personal data from our systems by contacting us, and we will comply with such requests where legally required.

6.3. **Withdrawal of Consent**: You can withdraw your consent for us to use your personal data at any time by discontinuing use of the bot. 

**7. Security**

We implement reasonable measures to ensure your data is stored securely and protected from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

**8. Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the bot’s functionality or applicable laws. You will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of the bot after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

**9. Contact Us**

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us via Discord or Patreon.
